After listening to more hör sets than I want to admit, I came up with a brilliant new idea.

Instead of using dj players on a set, use 2 (or more) grooveboxes and a dj mixer to perform. The groovebox I envisioned was some Elektron groovebox, so instead of songs the performer can perform with different patterns.

Of course this would change the techniques possible, and would not work with all genres, (i.e scratching in the traditional sense would be impossible), but for stuff like house or techno it might actually be better, as the performer would have a greater degree of freedom in fine-tuning which voices are active, and changing the sounds in real time. 

This would make the performer pretty busy, but I have yet to try this something like this, as I only have 1 groovebox advanced enough to make anything that would sound good enough for this kind of scenario. 

I realise that someone probably already does this, but I have not seen anyone do it in the format of a dj set.
